Employed technology

For facade and window cleaning, we utilize two methods. If the facade is traditionally soiled, the drone applies a special solution by foaming it onto the facade at low pressure. If the buildings have not been cleaned for a long time, or are extremely dirty, the process involves cleaning the facade and windows using the pure water method.

The pure water method simply involves using water treated with reverse osmosis, heated to 70 degrees, and applied under high pressure by the drone. This means that window cleaning is environmentally friendly because it is done without chemicals, and the windows stay cleaner over time. It also reduces the risk of surface damage, such as streaks caused by more traditional and hazardous manual cleaning methods.

Services details

Windows Cleaning

Commercial window cleaning at heights can be very time-consuming. We offer a new, more efficient, and safer way to provide high-rise window cleaning, which is considered faster, thereby reducing costs.

Modern buildings, including residential complexes, office buildings, and commercial properties in general, often have large glass surfaces. Due to their height, design, and location, performing regular maintenance can be time-consuming and costly. These buildings often require work at heights, which is expensive and carries risks.

Using a work drone is considered safe. Working at heights can be dangerous, and utilizing this drone technology eliminates the need for workers to physically enter these heights and hazardous environments. As a result, the foundations of this technology are considered much safer when working in hazardous environments.

Facade Cleaning

Until now, the cleaning of high-rise windows and facades has been carried out exclusively by professional cleaning companies specializing in rappelling, sky lifts, and, for smaller buildings, expensive scaffolding systems, cradles, or other lifting devices. High-rise window cleaning is considered one of the six most dangerous professions. Additionally, due to the costs involved, these tasks are performed less frequently, resulting in windows remaining dirty for longer periods.

Roof Washing

Drone roof washing offers safer, faster, and more efficient cleaning. Drones access hard-to-reach areas, reducing the risk of accidents and injuries. Precise control over water pressure and solutions ensures thorough cleaning without damaging roof materials, providing a cost-effective solution for maintaining clean roofs.

Solar Panels Cleaning

Solar panel drone washing offers efficient cleaning, maximizing panel performance. Drones access difficult areas, ensuring thorough cleaning without risk of damage. Regular cleaning removes dust and pollutants, preventing buildup that reduces energy efficiency. This method is cost-effective and environmentally friendly, maintaining optimal solar power generation.
