Drone washing

Until now, the cleaning of high-rise windows and facades has only been carried out by professional cleaning companies that specialize in abseiling, skylifts and for smaller buildings have implemented expensive scaffolding, use booms or other lifting equipment.

With the innovation and development of this solution by using drones eliminates the need for abseiling with rope access and lifts, making it safer, more efficient and cost effective more acceptable solution than traditional methods.

The benefits of using a work drone

  • Fast and efficient, 5-10 times faster than traditional methods

  • Easier access to hard-to-reach areas

  • Safer and more reliable.

  • No height equipment is required for access
